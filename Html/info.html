About Us

Welcome to My Sweet Music Store®, your one-stop destination for all things music. We are passionate about music and dedicated to providing musicians and music enthusiasts with the finest instruments, accessories, and expertise to help you make beautiful music.

Our Story

Founded in 1996, My Sweet Music Store® was born out of a love for music and a desire to share that passion with others. Our journey began in a small, humble space, and over the years, we've grown into a thriving music emporium, serving customers from all walks of life. From the very beginning, our mission has remained the same: to offer a wide selection of high-quality musical instruments and gear, excellent customer service, and a welcoming atmosphere that inspires creativity.

Our Commitment

At My Sweet Music Store®, we take pride in our commitment to our customers. We believe that music has the power to bring joy, inspire, and connect people from all backgrounds. Our dedicated team of knowledgeable and experienced staff is here to assist you in finding the perfect instrument or accessory, whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your musical journey.

We carefully curate our inventory to ensure that you have access to the latest and greatest products in the music industry. From guitars to keyboards, drums to microphones, and everything in between, we have the tools and equipment you need to express yourself through music.

Why Choose Us?

Expertise: Our staff is composed of musicians and music enthusiasts who can provide expert advice and guidance on your musical journey.

Quality: We carry products from renowned brands, ensuring that you're getting the best quality instruments and accessories.

Community: My Sweet Music Store® is more than just a store; it's a community of music lovers. Join us for events, workshops, and performances that celebrate the magic of music.

Service: Our friendly and attentive team is here to help you find the right equipment, make recommendations, and provide the support you need.

Visit Us Today

Whether you're a professional musician or a beginner, My Sweet Music Store® is here to support your musical aspirations. Visit our physical store, explore our online catalog, and let us be a part of your musical journey. Thank you for choosing us as your musical partner, and we look forward to being a part of your musical story.
